# Trademark Policy

Tezrisat is a project created and maintained by Emre Camkerten. This policy
explains how the Tezrisat name, logo, and branding may be used.

## What You May Do

- Use the Tezrisat name to describe unmodified copies of this project.
- Refer to Tezrisat in articles, videos, or tutorials that discuss the project.
- Use the name to link back to the official repository.

## What You May Not Do (Without Permission)

- Use the Tezrisat name or logo in a way that suggests endorsement,
  sponsorship, or affiliation.
- Use Tezrisat branding in a product or service name.
- Use the Tezrisat logo or name as part of a public hosted service.
- Register domains, social handles, or apps that are confusingly similar.

## Allowed Use for Forks

If you fork the project, please use a new name and branding for your fork.
You may state that your fork is "based on Tezrisat" but should not imply it
is the official project.
